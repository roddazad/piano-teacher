Additional Services

Music Composition

Learn the art of composing original music pieces and arrangements. Whether you're interested in classical, jazz, or contemporary styles, this course will help you develop your own unique musical voice. You will explore melody construction, harmony, and orchestration techniques.

Sight-Reading Training

Enhance your ability to read and play sheet music fluently. This course covers rhythm exercises, note recognition, and hands-on sight-reading practice to improve your speed and accuracy. Mastering this skill will help you become a more confident and adaptable musician.

Ear Training

Develop your ability to identify musical notes, chords, and rhythms by ear. This course will train you to recognize intervals, chord progressions, and melodic dictation, allowing you to play music more intuitively and improvise with greater confidence.

Improvisation Lessons

Learn how to create music on the spot and play freely without sheet music. Discover techniques used in jazz, blues, and classical improvisation. Gain confidence in experimenting with different scales, harmonies, and styles while developing your own musical creativity.

Studio Recording Techniques

Gain hands-on experience in recording, mixing, and producing piano music. This course covers microphone placement, sound engineering, and editing techniques to help you create professional-quality recordings of your performances or compositions.
